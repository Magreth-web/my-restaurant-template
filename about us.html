About Us

Fine Dining

Our Story

Established on the 14 Nvovember 2015,The Taste of Pleasures is the first ever project of Chef Adriano Kent and his wife Kaitlyn Kent. The Taste of pleasures is a neighborhood seasonal restaurant elevated by fine dining training and technique. Located in Swakopmund in the Theo Ben Gurirab Avenue, the dining room and bar are spacious with an open kitchen. The food at The Taste of Pleasures is rooted in African technique but draws inspiration from around the world, primarily the flavors from our childhoods, traveling, and the diverse cuisine in Swakopmund.
